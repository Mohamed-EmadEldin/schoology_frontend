We Are Schoology

Providing the best simple online school approach

At Schoology, we want to solve the biggest problem in online schooling:


Due to the global trend of digitizing education and in the midst of emerging pandemics, a need has arisen for online education and schooling.

Our Goal

1-To simulate the real-school daily interaction between student and teacher in the educational process.

2-To make the supervision of the child easier and more efficient .

3-To reduce the wasted days in the event of the need to work from home due to COVID high infection period or any other reason.

4-To make the process of administration easier and more efficient for the school.
